Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to common questions about booking with Wanderlust Escapes

How does the best price guarantee work?

If you find a lower publicly available price for the same travel itinerary within 24 hours of booking with us, we'll match the price and give you an additional 10% credit toward your next booking. Simply contact our customer support with the booking details and the lower price you found.

What is your cancellation policy?

Most bookings on Wanderlust Escapes offer free cancellation up to 24 hours before check-in or departure. However, cancellation policies vary by provider and rate type. The specific cancellation policy for each option is clearly displayed before you book. We always recommend reviewing this information carefully before confirming your reservation.

Do I need travel insurance?

While not mandatory, we strongly recommend travel insurance for all bookings. Unexpected events like flight cancellations, medical emergencies, or natural disasters can disrupt your plans. We offer comprehensive travel insurance options during the checkout process, or you can purchase your own. Our basic insurance is free for all bookings over $500.

How do personalized itineraries work?

After you book with us, you'll receive a questionnaire about your interests, travel style, and preferences. Our travel experts and AI system will then create a custom itinerary with recommendations for activities, dining, and local experiences tailored specifically to you. This service is complimentary for all bookings over $1000.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express, Discover), PayPal, Apple Pay, Google Pay, and bank transfers for certain larger bookings. All payments are processed through our secure, encrypted payment system to ensure your financial information is protected.
